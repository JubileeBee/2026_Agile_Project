# Planning Documents (Business Understanding Phase)

## Website Idea
A recipe-based website that enables users to create, share, and discover food content. The platform allows users to explore a wide range of meal ideas while engaging with a community of creators.

Users will be able to:
- Like and unlike recipes
- Comment on posted meals
- View ingredients used in recipes
- Create and manage wishlists
- View high-quality images of meals

---

## Website Naming
**CozyCravings**

The name “Cozy” conveys warmth, comfort, and a welcoming atmosphere, while “Cravings” directly relates to food and desire. Together the name communicates the platform’s purpose, of warmly welcoming our users to their food(y) cravings.

The alliteration (C.C.) makes the name memorable and easy to pronounce, contributing to our target of curating a premium and lifestyle-oriented brand identity.

## User Stories
User stories guide the design and development process by helping identify key user needs and use cases.

**Below are 10 user scenarios identified during early planning:**

- As a chef, I want to view highly rated meals so I can consider adding them to my restaurant menu.
- As a single parent, I want quick 15-minute recipes so I can prepare meals efficiently.
- As a university student, I want simple 3–4 ingredient meals to save time and money.
- As a personal cook, I want access to gourmet and organic recipes to enhance my offerings.
- As a content creator, I want to track followers and likes so I can optimise my content.
- As an elderly user, I want an intuitive interface so I can navigate the platform easily.
- As a family, we want easy-to-follow recipes suitable for children.
- As a FIFO worker, I want a visually clear interface so I can quickly find meals.
- As a parent, I want to see highly rated meals so I can prepare quality dishes.
- As a bodybuilder, I want macronutrient information so I can meet my dietary goals.
- As a doctor, I want to identify meals that may conflict with patient allergies.
