FLUFFY PANCAKES

★★★★☆ 4.5
♥ 128
DESSERT
Prep time 15 min
Cook time 15 min
Servings 8

Preparation

Whisk the egg yolk with a tablespoon of sugar until pale and frothy. Mix the milk in batches. Sift flour and baking powder over the mixture.

Whip egg whites until stiff peaks form. Fold gently into batter. Heat a non-stick pan and cook over low heat until golden.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ tbsp milk
  • ¼ cup flour
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Fresh berries
Notes:

Serve warm with butter, cream, and maple syrup.
